Getting Support

Support is available from the following sources depending on your commercial relationship:

  • If you are an app developer or organisation with a pre-existing relationship with Wincor Nixdorf, please email the Wincor Nixdorf AEVI SDK Team at support@aevi.com
  • If you are an app developer or organisation with no existing relationship with Wincor Nixdorf, please contact the bank, retailer or acquirer with whom you are proposing to market your app.
